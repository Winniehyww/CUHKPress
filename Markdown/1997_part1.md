## 中藥的前途繫於質量保證 香港中文大學展示中成藥檢測方法和儀器 | CUHK
Demonstrates Quality Control Methods for Common Chinese Medicine
**Date:** 1997-01-04
**Content:**
香港中文大學中藥研究中心將參加香港科技展覽97，展示檢測中成藥的方法和儀器，包括高效液相色譜儀辨別真假花旗參茶、原子吸收光譜儀分析牛黃解毒片中重金屬含量，以及DNA指紋技術鑑定藥材真偽。中心主任培曦教授強調，中藥質量保證對行業發展至關重要，近年事故如澳門牛黃解毒片砷超標凸顯其必要性。
The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) will demonstrate methods and
equipment for testing Chinese medicines at the Hong Kong Technology
Exhibition 97, including HPLC for American Ginseng Tea authenticity,
atomic absorption spectroscopy for arsenic in Niuhuang Jiedu Pien, and
DNA fingerprinting for herb authentication. Professor Paul But
highlights the critical role of quality assurance in sustaining the
industry.
**Labels:** #ResearchInnovation #MedicalScience #UniversityGovernance

## 香港中文大學校長李國章教授獲委為葛蘭素威康非執行董事 | CUHK
Vice-Chancellor Appointed Non-Executive Director of Glaxo Wellcome
**Date:** 1997-01-05
**Content:**
香港中文大學校長李國章教授獲葛蘭素威康集團委任為非執行董事，為首位亞洲人擔任此職。他畢業於劍橋大學，曾任職哈佛大學醫學院，1996年起任中大校長。葛蘭素威康為全球最大藥廠，年研發支出達12億英鎊。
Professor Arthur K.C. Li, Vice-Chancellor of CUHK, has been appointed a
Non-Executive Director of Glaxo Wellcome, the first Asian in this role.
A Cambridge graduate with experience at Harvard, he became CUHK
Vice-Chancellor in 1996. Glaxo Wellcome, the world’s largest
pharmaceutical company, invests £1.2 billion annually in R&D.
**Labels:** #Leadership #MedicalScienceIndustry #InternationalEventCollaboration

## 香港中文大學材料技術研究中心配置先進物料分析儀器 | CUHK Acquires New
High Energy Accelerator for Material Research
**Date:** 1997-01-10
**Content:**
香港中文大學材料技術研究中心安裝價值840萬港元的串列式加速器，為香港及華南地區獨有設備，用於離子束分析技術如RBS、核反應分析等，應用於半導體研究及古董鑑定，推動微電子與光學材料發展。
CUHK’s Materials Technology Research Centre installed an HK$8.4 million
tandem accelerator, unique in Hong Kong and South China, for ion beam
analysis (e.g., RBS) in semiconductor research, antique analysis, and
microelectronic/optical material development.
