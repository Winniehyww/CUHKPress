# New Mechanical Engineering Programme on Offer at the Chinese
University of Hong Kong
**Date:** 1993-10-11
**Content:**
The Chinese University of Hong Kong’s Faculty of Engineering will launch
a new undergraduate Mechanical Engineering programme in September 1994.
It covers robotics, mechatronics, and computer-aided design, with a
three-year curriculum leading to a Bachelor’s degree. Applications are
via JUPAS, deadline November 1, 1993.
