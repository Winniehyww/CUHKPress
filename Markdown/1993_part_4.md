# CUHK to hold 30th Anniversary Concert
**Date:** 1993-03-19
**Content:**
The Chung Chi Choir of the Chinese University of Hong Kong will hold a
30th Anniversary Concert on March 29 at 8:00 pm at City Hall Concert
Hall. Conducted by Dr. Victor Chan, it features works by Faure, Franck,
Liszt, Chinese songs from the 1930s-40s, and Kodaly’s "Pange Lingua"
(Hong Kong premiere). Prof. David Gwilt and Mrs. Marilyn Watson will
perform Poulenc’s "Sonata for Two Pianos". Tickets: $40-$100, half-price
for students and seniors.
