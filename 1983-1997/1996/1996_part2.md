## 中大醫學院與加拿大亞伯達大學醫學院簽署學術交流協議 | Chinese
University of Hong Kong Signs Medical Faculty Exchange Agreement with
University of Alberta in Canada
**Date:** 1996-02-07
**Content:**
香港中文大學醫學院與加拿大亞伯達大學醫學院於2月7日在威爾斯親王醫院簽署交流協議，促進教學、研究及臨床合作。每年互派數名資深學者進行六週交流，提升雙方醫學水平。亞伯達大學創於1908年，其醫學院在加拿大執照考試中名列前茅，研究經費近半來自外部捐助。
The CUHK Faculty of Medicine and the University of Alberta Faculty of
Medicine signed an exchange agreement on February 7 at Prince of Wales
Hospital, fostering teaching, research, and clinical collaboration.
Senior staff exchange for six weeks annually to enhance medical
standards. Founded in 1908, Alberta’s Faculty excels in licensing exams,
with nearly 50% of research funding from external sources.
**Labels:** #MedicalEducation #InternationalCollaboration
#UniversityAgreement

**Date:** 1996-02-07
**Content:**
香港中文大學外科學系於2月9日至11日在香港會議展覽中心主辦第二屆亞太燒傷會議。開幕禮由醫管局行政總裁楊永強主持，嘉賓包括世界衛生組織及國際燒傷學會代表。中大將宣布亞洲首次成功應用人造皮（Integra）治療燒傷患者的突破。

## 燒傷治理的重大進展 | Major Advances in Burn Care
**Date:** 1996-02-09
**Content:**
中大外科學系主辦第二屆亞太燒傷會議，宣布香港首次成功應用人造皮治療兩名燒傷患者（25%及50%燒傷面積），為亞洲首創。會議探討燒傷管理、皮膚替代物及預防，顯示香港63%燒傷發生家中，52%涉及12歲以下兒童。
CUHK’s Surgery Department hosted the 2nd Asian-Pacific Burns Conference,
announcing Hong Kong’s first successful use of artificial skin on two
burn patients (25% and 50% body surface), a first in Asia. Topics
included burn management, skin substitutes, and prevention, noting 63%
of HK burns occur at home, 52% involving kids under 12.
**Labels:** #MedicalInnovation #BurnResearch #ConferenceHighlight
