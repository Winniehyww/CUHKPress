# Conference on the Development of Contemporary China Studies
**Date:** 1993-06-25
**Content:**
China experts from the US, UK, Australia, France, Japan, and Hong Kong
convened on June 25 at the Furama Kempinski Hotel for an international
conference on contemporary China studies, hosted by CUHK’s Universities
Service Centre for its 30th anniversary. Speakers like Prof. Ezra Vogel
and Prof. Gilbert Rozman discussed global China studies trends and the
impact of China’s reforms.

# 香港中文大學醫學院精神科學系公佈「沙田心理健康調查」結果 | THE SHATIN
COMMUNITY MENTAL HEALTH SURVEY
**Date:** 1993-06-29
**Content:**
香港中文大學醫學院精神科學系公布1984-86年「沙田心理健康調查」結果，首次在港使用美國診斷問卷，調查7000多人。顯示常見心理疾病如煙草依賴（男26.6%）、泛發性焦慮症（女11.1%），建議加強社區心理健康服務。
