## 中大成功使用準分子激光手術治療角膜疾病 | CUHK Introduces Laser
Technology to Treat Corneal Diseases
**Date:** 1997-04-10 (merged with 1997-04-11 entries)
**Content:**
香港中文大學眼科及視覺科學學系成功使用「光治療角膜切除術」（PTK）準分子激光手術治療角膜疾病，去除疤痕並平滑角膜表面，緩解疼痛，部分患者免除角膜移植。另一技術「LASIK」則治療深度近視。4月11日舉行記者會介紹技術，患者分享經驗；4月12-13日在威爾斯親王醫院舉辦國際研討會及實習課程。
CUHK’s Department of Ophthalmology & Visual Sciences successfully
applied Phototherapeutic Keratectomy (PTK) laser surgery to treat
corneal diseases, removing scars and smoothing surfaces, reducing
transplant needs. LASIK treats severe myopia. A press conference on
April 11 introduced the techniques, with patients sharing experiences,
followed by an international symposium and training on April 12-13 at
Prince of Wales Hospital.
**Labels:** #MedicalBreakthrough #EyeHealth #ResearchInnovation

## 香港中文大學積極籌辦中醫學士學位課程 | CUHK Proposes to Offer
Bachelor of Chinese Medicine Programme
**Date:** 1997-04-10
**Content:**
香港中文大學計劃於1998-99學年開辦三年制中醫學士課程，首年招收15名學生，結合理論與實習，培養能提供初級醫療的中醫師。課程利用醫學院及中藥研究中心資源，旨在提升香港中醫教育水平。
CUHK plans to launch a three-year Bachelor of Chinese Medicine program
in 1998-99, admitting 15 students initially, integrating theory and
practice to train primary care practitioners, leveraging its Faculty of
Medicine and Chinese Medicinal Material Research Centre.
**Labels:** #EducationProgram #MedicalTraining #ChineseMedicine

## 香港中文大學再有三項研究計劃獲評為卓越 | CUHK Leads in Number of
Excellent Research Projects
**Date:** 1997-04-14
**Content:**
香港中文大學三項研究獲研究資助局評為「卓越」，總數增至15項，居本地院校之首。包括梁怡教授的智能空間決策支援系統（應用於洪水模擬）、李卓予與馮國培教授的高熱抗癌研究、以及曼與容永豪教授的感覺受體細胞研究。
Three CUHK research projects were rated “Excellent” by the Research
Grants Council, raising the total to 15, the highest among local
institutions. Projects include Prof. Leung Yee’s intelligent spatial
decision support system (flood simulation), Profs. Lee and Fung’s
hyperthermia cancer research, and Profs. Baumann and Yung’s sensory
receptor study.
**Labels:** #ResearchInnovation #ScienceAchievement
#UniversityExcellence

## 中國著名核物理學家王淦昌教授蒞港 主講二十一世紀主要能源展望 | Public
Lecture by Distinguished Nuclear Physicist Professor Wang Ganchang
**Date:** 1997-04-16 (merged with 1997-04-17 and 1997-04-18 entries)
**Content:**
中國核物理學家王淦昌教授於4月18日在香港中文大學及何梁何利基金主辦的講座中，以「21世紀能源展望」為題，探討化石燃料缺陷、核燃料應用及中國現況，並分享其慣性約束聚變研究成就。講座於中環恒生銀行總行舉行。

**Date:** 1997-04-18 (merged with 1997-04-19 and 1997-04-23 entries)
**Content:**
香港中文大學崇基學院與台灣東華大學合辦「社會科學的應用與中國現代化研討會」，4月22日開幕由費孝通教授主講，探討華人社會現代化問題。研討會分兩階段：4月22日在香港，4月23-30日在台灣，匯聚40位兩岸專家。

seminar, opened by Prof. Fei Xiaotong on April 22, addressing
modernization issues in Chinese societies. Held in Hong Kong (April 22)
and Taiwan (April 23-30), it featured 40 experts.

## 香港中文大學乳癌療法及研究新發展 | CUHK Advances in Breast Cancer
Treatment and Research
**Date:** 1997-04-25
**Content:**
中大與國際乳癌研究組合作，試驗高劑量化療配原細胞支援治療乳癌，並研究華裔女性的BRCA1基因突變，助早期診斷。調查顯示本地女性乳癌知識不足，4月26-27日舉辦國際會議分享進展。
CUHK collaborates with the International Breast Cancer Study Group to
test high-dose chemotherapy with stem cell support and studies BRCA1
mutations in Chinese women for early diagnosis. A survey highlighted
knowledge gaps, with advances shared at a conference on April 26-27.
